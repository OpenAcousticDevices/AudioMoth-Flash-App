Connect the AudioMoth device
Set the switch on the side of your AudioMoth device to DEFAULT, remove the device's batteries and SD card, and then connect it to your computer via USB.
Both LEDs should start to flash.
Power into flash mode
Let's try starting your AudioMoth directly into flash mode. Disconnect the USB cable and use a metal paperclip to connect the two contacts marked with the white band.
With the contacts connected, reattach the USB cable.
Enable flash mode
Use a metal paperclip to connect the two contacts marked with the white band.
Be sure to hold the paperclip tightly against the contacts.
Hold until the LEDs stop flashing.
Verify connection.
If done correctly, the device should enter a flashable state (shown below) and the paperclip can be removed.
Current status:
Not in flashable state.
Set switch.
Finally, set switch to “USB/OFF” and you’re ready to flash your device.
